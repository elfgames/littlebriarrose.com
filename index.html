LITTLE BRIAR ROSE

Little Briar Rose is a point-and-click adventure game inspired by “The Sleeping Beauty”

A brave prince is trying to save his princess from an evil spell that forces her to sleep forever. In order to save her, he has to explore the enchanted forest which surrounds the castle where she is imprisoned.
